Sensitive

"Sensitive wine and chocolate" brand represents sensitive sensations in chocolate and wine. The Scottish brand operates as a specialty wine and chocolate store. The activities of the store include music. When you buy any product from the store, you are given a QR code on the label. When you scan this code, you listen to the music that was given to you. You can choose the music you want. The brand is divided into 3 styles: night, sun and sunset. The feelings represented by the 3 styles are shown to you through illustrations. Red wine represents the harshness of the night, white wine represents the warmth of the sun and rose wine represents the softness of the sunset. In chocolates, mint chocolate represents a cool night, dark chocolate represents a warm sun, and milk chocolate represents a soft sunset.
